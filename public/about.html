Who We Are

AEON Travels & Tours is a Sri Lanka-based travel company specialising in bespoke, tailor-made holidays across Asia. We connect travellers with deeply knowledgeable local experts who go beyond the guidebook to show you the real heart of each destination.

Our team handles everything — flights, accommodation, transfers, activities, and more — so you can focus entirely on the experience. Whether it's a romantic honeymoon in the Maldives, a cultural deep-dive through Vietnam, or a family adventure in Sri Lanka, we design every trip around you.

We believe great travel is personal. That's why no two AEON itineraries are ever the same.
