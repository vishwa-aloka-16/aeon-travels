Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to reply to all enquiries within 24 hours. During busy periods WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us — we're available around the clock.

Is there a fee for getting a quote?

Absolutely not. All quotes and consultations are completely free with no obligation. We only charge once you're happy with your itinerary and confirm your booking.

Can I customise a package I see on the site?

Yes — every package is a starting point. We tailor every detail to your preferences, timeline, and budget. Nothing is off the table.

Do you handle visas and travel insurance?

We provide full guidance on visa requirements for all our destinations and can recommend trusted insurance providers, though final arrangements remain with the traveller.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, major credit and debit cards, and PayPal. A deposit secures your booking, with the balance due closer to your departure date.

What if I need to change or cancel my trip?

We understand plans change. Our team will work with you to amend or reschedule your trip wherever possible. Full cancellation terms are provided at the time of booking.
